About Me

Hello! I am a scientists who’s interests span several topics including the neuroscience, control of behavior (specifically breathing and airway protective behaviors), learning, spinal cord injury, computational modeling, statistics, and data visualization. I hope that my research will improve the health and wellbeing of humans and animals.
